# LeadFlow AI 🚀

AI-powered sales lead qualification and automation platform built using Flask, OpenRouter LLM APIs, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

## Overview
LeadFlow AI helps sales teams automate inbound lead qualification by analyzing lead information such as company size, budget, industry, and business requirements.

The system uses LLM-based AI analysis to:

- Score leads from 0–100
- Classify leads as Hot / Warm / Cold
- Generate business summaries
- Recommend next sales actions
- Automatically draft professional follow-up emails
- Export downloadable lead analysis reports
